About Me?

A Little Something About Myself!

Tapping Tool Mechanism

An assignment where I had to create a tool tapping mechanism. Tool Tapping mechanism are used to give internal threads to a component/workpiece. This was created using the software Autodesk Inventor. Here I used the basic and standard commands including Threading, Rotation.

A Standard Thermo Kraft heater

Just another "let's not head out, it's too cold and windy" type of day in Berlin. Decided to design myself a heater. I have made use of commands such as extrude, revolve, mirror, sketch pattern, extrude drafting, shell, sweep and sweep-cut and mad use of sheet metal for the design of the heater.

Basic Steel Table

Designed a simple basic rectangular tube table of 120 x 80 x 8 to cover some basic weldments. Used weld beads to weld gussets to table frame. These tables are mostly seen in Mechanical Workshops and Industries to hold Machine Wise, Drilling, Milling and heavy machinery.

CFD simulations of conformal cooing channels

Thermal and fluid analysis (CFD) of conformal cooling channels in molds to improve life of molds, thermal temperature and optimization of cooling from standard straight drilled cooling channels. 3D printed a prototype using the software Cura and Ultimaker s5.
