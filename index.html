A Little Something About Myself!

Hands-on experience in machine and equipment design used in the industry for mass manufacturing and custom machining. CFD Analysis of conformal cooling channels using 3D manufacturing technqiues

Conducted thermal and fluid (CFD) analysis on 3D-manufactured molds with conformal cooling channels for plastic injection molding. This study demonstrates enhanced cooling efficiency, reduced cycle times, and improved product quality, showcasing the advantages of additive manufacturing in optimizing mold performance.
